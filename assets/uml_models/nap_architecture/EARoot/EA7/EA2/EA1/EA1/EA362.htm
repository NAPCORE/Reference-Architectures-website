The platform shall follow at least 3 of the below defined usability rules:<br/>a. Menus are in the same position<br/>b. Use of same fonts and colours throughout the site<br/>c. Availability of a search box on the main page (search means for navigation along the webpage and no data discovery)<br/>d. Logo links back to the home page<br/>e. Existence of a hierarchical arrangement of web subpages (“breadcrumbs” type also means hierarchical arrangement)<br/>f. Existence of a “sitemap” page easing navigation (e.g., a tree structure with links to individual pages)<br/><br/>Source: KPI 1.7 Web performance – Consistency and navigability<br/>
