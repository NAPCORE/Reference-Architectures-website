<b>7.2 “Terms and conditions for data reuse from data providers”</b> KPI assesses, from the perspective of data provider, whether a data provider provides descriptive/detailed information about the terms and conditions of data advertised by the data provider at the NAP. A value equal to 0 points out an absence of such a provision, a value equal to 1 indicates that there is a descriptive provision, while a value equal to 2 indicates that there is a detailed provision through sample contracts or standardized data licensing frameworks such as CC – (Creative Commons) License. It is assumed that a NAP should at least mandate detailed provision through sample contracts or standardized data licensing frameworks about the terms and conditions for data reuse.<br/>The difference between 7.1 "<b> Terms and conditions for reuse of metadata and NAP website content </b>" and 7.2 " Terms and conditions for data reuse from data providers " KPIs lies in perspective: 7.1 assesses whether the <i>NAP operator</i> provides clear information on terms and conditions for data reuse, while 7.2 evaluates whether <i>data providers</i> publishing on the NAP offer similar clarity regarding their data reuse terms.<br/>Note: detailed terms and conditions means e.g. full sample contract conditions and/or standardized licenses framework such as “CC (Creative Commons) License”)<br/>Values:<br/><b>0:</b> No<br/><b>1:</b> Descriptive (brief text description)<br/><b>2:</b> Detailed terms and conditions can be provided for datasets<br/><b>3: </b>Provision of<b> </b>detailed terms and conditions is mandatory for all published data sets<br/><b>4: </b>Provision of<b> </b>detailed terms and conditions is mandatory for all published data sets using standardized license framework<br/><br/>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 4<br/>
