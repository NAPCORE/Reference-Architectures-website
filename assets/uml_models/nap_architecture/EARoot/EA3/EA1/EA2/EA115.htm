This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/> (1) The ability to receive and process support requests from any existing or potential NAP Content Provider or NAP Content Consumer.<br/> (2) The ability to respond to user support requests adequately at least in a form of first-level support.<br/> (3) The ability to consult other NAP roles for issues which cannot be responded with the own knowledge.<br/> (4) The ability to manage issues and log the issue resolution for later use by NAP Users or by an Auditing entity<br/><br/>NOTE: this could be done via a ticketing system<br/>
