The purpose of compliance assessment is defined in the delegated acts established under the European ITS Directive 2010/40/EU. The delegated acts also define the tasks of NBs/CAs to be nominated by Member States. To successfully start their tasks and to prepare for compliance assessment the NBs/CAs need to plan the execution of compliance assessment per Delegated Regulation on national level.<br/>
