The National Body / Competent Authority has the task to provide information to private and public stakeholders,<br/>organization, interested persons and answer any other related information request.<br/>Stakeholder:<br/>Stakeholder as main focus group can be informed about their tasks and obligations based on the ITS directive<br/>2010/40/EU and their delegated regulations which includes deadlines, of handing in self-declarations for self-assessments and random inspections. This information process also includes the offering of direct information meetings and other support activities.<br/>General information:<br/>The NB/CA should provide information about acting organizations, the geographical allocation of National Bodies, about National Body obligations, contact details, communication channels (website, e-Mail, hotline, etc) , information<br/>about the delegated regulations and regarded deadlines.<br/>Provision of information: It is recommended to provide the above-mentioned information via one website<br/>
