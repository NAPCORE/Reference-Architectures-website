The Terminator "National Access Point(s)" represents authorized National Access Points (NAPs) for the Delegated Regulations of the ITS Directive 2010/40/EU per Member State. The NAP has to provide information about registered stakeholders, registered data sets and related service publications. In the case that a NAP is a data portal, the NAP has to provide access to data or service content while a compliance assessment is performed.<br/>
