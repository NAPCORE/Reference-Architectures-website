The platform shall provide Mass notifications to registered providers in at least one of the following ways:<br/><ul>
<li>Mass notifications are possible for legal (T&C) updates</li><li>Mass notifications are tailored according to the data provider's needs, e.g. new requirements for data provision like data profile</li><li>Mass notifications are available on the NAP website to the registered data providers, e.g., pop-up information after logging in</li></ul>
