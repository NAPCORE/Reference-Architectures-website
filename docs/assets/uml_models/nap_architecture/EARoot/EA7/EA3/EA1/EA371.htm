<b>Type</b>: Legal<br/><b>Full Name: </b>Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1012 of 7 April 2022 supplementing Regulation (EC) No 561/2006 of the European Parliament and of the Council with regard to the establishment of standards detailing the level of service and security of safe and secure parking areas and to the procedures for their certification<br/><b>Link:</b> <a href="file://https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg_del/2022/1012/oj"><font color="#467886"><u>https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg_del/2022/1012/oj</u></font></a> <br/><b>Description</b>: This Regulation supplements Regulation (EC) No 561/2006 on driving and rest times in road transport. It establishes harmonised EU standards for the level of service, security, and certification procedures for Safe and Secure Truck Parking Areas (SSTPAs). The goal is to improve driver safety, working conditions, and cargo protection across the EU.<br/><b>Additional details</b>: While the regulation does not explicitly mandate publication via NAPs, it is highly relevant where parking area data (e.g. location, security level, available services) is made available to users through ITS services. Under Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/1926, such data must be accessible via National Access Points to support multimodal travel information services. <br/><b>Related Documents:</b><br/><ul>
<li><a href="file://https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2006/561/"><font color="#467886"><u>Regulation (EC) No 561/2006</u></font></a> – The parent regulation on driving and rest times; provides the legal basis for requiring safe rest areas for professional drivers.</li><li><a href="file://https://eu-parkings.eu/"><font color="#467886"><u>EU SSTPA Standard Website</u></font></a> – Maintained by the European Commission Expert Group on SSTPAs; provides technical details, certification criteria, and implementation support.</li></ul><p/>
