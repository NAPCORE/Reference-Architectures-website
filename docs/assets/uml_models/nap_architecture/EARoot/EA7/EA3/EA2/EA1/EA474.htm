The "Homepage load time" assesses the time response of a NAP’s website by assessing the time in seconds needed to load a homepage. Tools are now added and some recommendations how to use them for this KPI.<br/>Please, measure load time from 3 different European cities other than your country, in daytime (8-16h), and make an arithmetic mean of the results. Use one or both of the following free services:<br/><a href="file://https://www.uptrends.com/tools/website-speed-test"><font color="#0563c1"><u>https://www.uptrends.com/tools/website-speed-test</u></font></a><br/><a href="file://https://tools.pingdom.com/"><font color="#0563c1"><u>https://tools.pingdom.com/</u></font></a><br/><br/>Values:<br/><b>0: </b>higher than 4 sec<br/><b>1: </b>higher than 2 sec, max. 4 sec<br/><b>2: </b>higher than 1 sec, max. 2 sec<br/><b>3: </b>higher than 0.5 sec, max. 1 sec<br/><b>4: </b>max. 0.5 sec<br/><br/><b>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 2</b><br/>
