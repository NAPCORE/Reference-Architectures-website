<b>5.1 “Documentation & description of datasets”</b> KPI assesses whether the datasets of a NAP are accompanied by supporting material, such as documentation, high-level descriptions (included in a metadata filed), XML schemas or samples, or even further detailed documentation. The minimum level of service is associated with the provision of XML schemas or samples. In case there are available links with additional information about datasets, an advanced level of service is assumed.<br/><br/>Values:<br/><b>0: </b>Lack of dataset documentation and description<b> </b><br/><b>1: </b>High-level description of datasets including standard metadata elements<br/><b>2:</b> Availability of links and supporting material in the form of XML schemas and samples, where necessary<br/><b>3:</b> High level description of datasets including standard metadata elements, XML schemas and samples, and further supporting material such as detailed documentation and protocol documentation <br/><br/><br/>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 2<br/><br/>
