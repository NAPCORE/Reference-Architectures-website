<b>Operational procedure information:</b> KPI assesses whether a NAP includes guidelines and operational procedures that need to be followed for becoming a data provider or data consumer of a NAP or, general responsibilities (who is responsible for doing what within or while using the NAP). In this case, the ACC value is set to 1 reflecting that NAPs provide such guidelines.<br/><i><br/></i><i>Note: Examples of operational procedure information:</i><br/><ul>
<li><i>Terms and conditions for use of the NAP,</i></li><li><i>Governance aspects of NAP,</i></li><li><i>Processes for data quality assessment and maintenance,</i></li><li><i>How a potential data provider/consumer becomes accredited,</i></li><li><i>How the data is provided,</i></li><li><i>Where to discuss the technical issues</i></li></ul>
<i><br/></i><i><br/></i>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 1<br/>
